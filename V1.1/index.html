About Me

While working in product development, I was given a project that involved managing the creation of an app....

That’s when I got hooked.

Learning all the practical stages that go into creating an app was riveting. I would end up accidentally working late, absorbed in making mockups and wireframes. Though as a chemist, I was the project manager, not the developer and therefore had to leave the app with the professionals.

So, I decided to dive a little deeper into the world of code, to dust off my old HTML knowledge (circa 2011) and see what other languages were waiting for me.
